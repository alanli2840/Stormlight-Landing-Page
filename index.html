Welcome to the Stormlight Archives

The Stormlight Archive is a modern epic fantasy series written by one of the most prolific speculative fiction writers currently, Brandon Sanderson. It is set to span across ten novels with two major story-arcs across five books each. As of 2023, there are four of the ten books already published along with two novellas, each being highly acclaimed. The first novel, The Way of Kings, was published on August 31, 2010 with the fifth and final book of the current story arc being slated to be released sometime in 2024.

Sanderson's Magnum Opus

The Stormlight Archives is Sanderson's most praised work, situated in the world of Roshar. This world boasts a vibrant ecosystem, a society of constantly developing technology, and a magic system that despite defying all common sense, is built upon a surprisingly solid foundation of otherworldly logic. The Stormlight Archives is truly a series that you don't want to miss out on. It is a legacy in the making.
